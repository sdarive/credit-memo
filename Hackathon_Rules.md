Here are the key rules and guidelines for the Financial AI Hackathon Championship 2025, as presented by LandingAI:

***

## Eligibility & Participation

- **Open to everyone**—students, professionals, and builders from any background.
- Remote submissions are welcome. The Top 10 finalist teams will be invited to attend and present in-person in New York City on November 15, 2025.
- Register before **Nov 9, 11:59 PM ET** to participate (registration is via [Luma](https://luma.com/jme15h1t?referrer=luma)).
- You can start building immediately after registering.

***

## Build Timeline

- **Building is open now**.
- **Submission deadline:** Nov 10, 11:59 PM ET (via [Google Form](https://forms.gle/q682wg7ZWLnNUqQL6)).
- Finalists will be invited to pitch live on November 15, 10 AM – 1 PM ET in NYC.

***

## Required Stack & Solution Guidelines

- Solutions **must use**:
  - **LandingAI Agentic Document Extraction (ADE)** API (mandatory).
  - **Any LLMs** (AWS Bedrock supported, free credits available).
  - **Any agentic framework** (your choice; e.g., custom workflow, orchestration, or tools like Replit, Lovable, etc.).
- **Free ADE credits** are provided upon signing up for [va.landing.ai](https://va.landing.ai/my/playground/ade). Additional credits available via Discord if needed.
- **Do NOT build "anti-projects,"** i.e., projects outside finance, AI chatbots for education, health, job screening, basic RAG apps, Streamlit-only apps, nutrition/personality/mental health coaches, etc.—these are grounds for immediate disqualification.

***

## Project Scope

- Build intelligent financial agents for real-world doc processing and financial analysis.
- Demo must cover: upload of complex financial documents, ADE integration, and agentic analysis (e.g., memo generation, due diligence, risk summary, etc.).
- Usability, transparency, and workflow design are important for judging.

***

## Judging Criteria

- **Problem clarity and domain relevance**
- **Depth of ADE integration and technical implementation**
- **Accuracy, reliability, and performance on real documents**
- **Usability and workflow design**
- **Feasibility and pilot path within 90 days**
- **Quality and clarity of 4-minute demo/presentation**

***

## Prizes

- **Champion:** $5,000 ($3K cash, $2K ADE credits)
- **Honorable Mention:** $1,000 cash
- **Best Online-Only App:** Surprise prize + signed swag (US shipping only)

***

## Submission & Event Details

- Submit by Nov 10 via the provided Google Form.
- Final event NOV 15, 10 AM–1 PM ET (NYC).
- Teams are responsible for their travel/accommodation.

***

## Support & Resources
